Smashed Boiled Eggs with Feta Cheese Pita

Description

A healthy high protein salad or a delicious sandwich

Ingredients

  • 3 hard-boiled eggs
  • 45g of creamy feta
  • pita bread
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a small pot over high heat, bring a couple of centimeters of water to boil
  2. place eggs into the water gently, lower the heat to low and cover the pot
  3. Let the eggs cook covered for 10 minutes then place in ice-water to peel
  4. Cut the eggs into a bowl with the feta and smash everything down rougly with a fork
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste and serve (or make sandwiches with pita)
