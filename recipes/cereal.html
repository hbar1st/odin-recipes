Cereal With Milk & Banana

Description

Sweet tasting cereal recipe without added sugars

Ingredients

  • Fibre 1 Crunchy Bran Cereal, 1/3 cup
  • Banana, cut in half, then sliced
  • Milk 2%, 250 ml

Steps

  1. In cereal bowl, measure out bran
  2. Add banana to the bowl
  3. Pour in milk and mix
